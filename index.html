Neston Drama Group

Founded in 1977, our aim is to provide high quality amateur theatre for the people of Neston and surrounding areas.

Our productions range from comedy and farce to thrillers and serious drama.

We have 2 productions a year, one in May and one in November, so please feel free to come along!
